# NRS-Tune-Library
Northern Roots Session tune library in ABC format

Though we'll play just about anything someone brings in, Northern Roots has a core repertoire of tunes. The list below contains most of them, and provides an easy way for you to download sheet music or listen to the tunes to help with the learning process.

Note: we learn most of our tunes by ear, so they sometimes often come out sounding a little different from the "standard" versions found here. If you learn a tune from here, don't be surprised if we put some of the notes in different places.

These tunes are stored in a simple format called abc, which is designed to be easy to edit without special tools. You can download the abc file containing all of the tunes to work with on your own computer. There's also a file of retired tunes that used to be on this page but aren't played anymore.

## About the Northern Roots Session

Northern Roots Session is an informal weekly gathering of musicians and friends who love to play acoustic music with roots in the north, particularly the nordic countries. Participants and listeners at all ages and levels of experience are always welcome. The musical environment is supportive, non-competitive, and generally very laid back.

Our weekly music jam session occurs on Thursday evenings, 7:00-9:00ish, at The Contented Cow, a friendly Welsh-style pub that serves a wide range of beverages at 302B Division Street South in Northfield, MN. All ages are welcome.

Some members of our group occasionally perform at events around the community as Hütenänny. We generally donate our performance fees to local charities.

Our usual complement of instruments include — but are not limited to:

* Fiddles, including regular fiddle, Norwegian hardanger fiddle, Swedish nyckelharpa (keyed) fiddle), viola
* Guitars, mandolins, banjos, autoharps, harpeleik (Norwegian chorded zither), bass
* Accordions, including various piano accordion, 2-row, 3-row and concertinas
* Recorder, tin whistle
* Voice

Please join us!
